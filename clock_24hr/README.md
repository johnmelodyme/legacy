# 24-Hour Clock

Art project.

Its dial rotates once every 24 hours.  At its alarm time, it plays The Nutcracker.  An old GPS provides a 1HZ time oscillator.  A BASIC Stamp 1 counts the pulses and drives a stepper motor.
